Our Mission

The 200 Club of Hawaii is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing immediate financial support to the families of Honolulu Police Department and Honolulu Fire Department officers and firefighters who make the ultimate sacrifice in the line of duty. When tragedy strikes, we ensure that no family faces the burden of loss alone.

"When a hero falls, we stand with their family."
